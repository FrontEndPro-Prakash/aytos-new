AYTOS Network

AYTOS Network is a virtual city that comprises of assets that we will be acquiring across North America. Our initial focus will be the U.S. market and Canada, and expanding to Asia and Europe..

  • Assets : Our assets will mainly be Single Family Homes, Condominiums, and Apartment. We want to build a top tier hotel experience using these assets as a foundation to provide a new living standard to everyone.
  • Business Model : Our plan is to acquire a group existing properties and remodel those assets to includes amenities and a design that are comparable to a hotel. We want to introduce hotel living quality to long-term / short-term, business and vacation customers or tenants, and we will run the entire operations on the blockchain. All of our residents (customers and tenants) will be connected via a technology protocol in the AYTOS Network. This technology will be the essential communication channel for sharing information, pictures, video, requesting services, and placing orders to the surrounding retailers or restaurants.

AYTOS Platform

The AYTOS Platform is a TechnologyPeer-2-Peer (“P2P”) Platform that is aiming to change the process in acquiring and investing in Real Estate. We are going to tokenizing and digitizing the real estate industry, and eliminating all the middle - man. We believe acquiring and investing in real estates should be straight-forward and simple.

  • Platform Business Model : Our business model is to create a platform where anyone in the world can invest in real estate with as little as $250. Our model will break a real estate into blocks and each block will represent $250 of the investment. Each of these blocks are also entitled to the income generated from the asset. We want to open up Real Estate income opportunities to everyone.
  • How the Platform Works : Sara put her home up for sale. In the current environment, Sara would need to pay 5-6% fees to her Real Estate Agent. With the AYTOS platform, Sara will be paying 2-3% in fees and the entire transaction will performed over the blockchain. Worldwide investors subscribe to purchase Sara’s asset by block and once fully subscribed, the transaction will trigger and complete via a Smart Contract. Upon the completion of the transaction, AYTOS will register the list of new owners to the property owner registration office at the county or state office where the assets is located.
