Who We Are

AYTOS is a Network and a Platform. Our Network operates hotel quality real estate properties on the blockchain. Our Platform is an industry breakthrough on the blockchain that gives worldwide investors the ability to invest in real estate with as little as $250.

Creating a city on the blockchain and having all the transactions perform on the blockchain
Dividend investment platform for crypto and micro investors worldwide
Building a platform on the blockchain that will change the way the real estate industry works
The Mission

AYTOS is creating a bridge for worldwide investors to gain access to the highly lucrative North American Real Estate market through the blockchain. Our thesis comprises of the AYTOS Network and AYTOS Platform

The Network

AYTOS Network will directly own stable, income-generating U.S. and Canadian residential and vacation real estate across key property types, including single family homes, condominium, multifamily, and vacation homes

The Platform

AYTOS Platform is a Peer-2-Peer ( “ P2P ” ) Technology that will open the attractive Real Estate industry to micro-investors worldwide, allowing anyone to get started with as little as $250. The Platform is also an exchange
